Due to the COVID-19 outbreak we've made the decision to postpone the workshop that was originally planned on the 16th of May 2020. We will continue to update about the situation on this page, on our @RailsGirls_NL Twitter account and through email if you've signed up as a participant or coach. If you have any questions about this, contact us through contact@railsgirls.nl.
