Ruby on Rails Workshop for Girls, 26th-27th, November 2010

Workshop

Designing and building web apps is not magic but result of applying techniques and knowledge.

The free workshop will go through the whole lifecycle of a modern web application from concept to code. During one weekend you will design, plan, build and deploy a real application together with other interested people.

The goal is to provide a great first experience on building web apps. You'll learn sketching, prototyping, basic programming paradigms and get a quick introduction to the world of Rails. The workshop is perfect for girls and women who want to take a look under the hood of technology and web development

Questions & Answers

Why are you organizing this?

Internet was built by boys for boys. Sometimes, as a girl, it might be intimidating to ask questions or get started.

Kisko loves web, Rails - and girls. We like to help people learn new things.

What do I need to know?

Just basic knowledge of working with a computer. Zero programming or web development experience required - we'll walk through the development process together in small teams.

HOW TO ATTEND FUTURE EVENTS?

Leave your e-mail and you'll get notified on future events on Rails.

Can men attend?

Yes, but you need to be accompanied by an interested lady.
