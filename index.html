Ruby on Rails Workshop for Girls, 26th-27th, November 2010

Workshop

Designing and building web apps is not magic but result of applying techniques and knowledge.

The workshop will go through the whole lifecycle of a modern web application. During the weekend, You will design, plan, build and deploy a real application together with other interested people.

The goal is to provide a great first experience on building web apps. The workshop is perfect for girls and women interested in learning more about technology and developing for the web.
