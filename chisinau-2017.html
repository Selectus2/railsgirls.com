Rails Girls Chișinău 13, 20 & 27 May 2017

Hi there!

Rails Girls comes to Chișinău for the third time! During the three-day workshop we'll dive into the magical world of Ruby on Rails. Let's get excited together!

Apply now! Applications are open until the 23rd of April 2017.

You learn designing, prototyping and coding with the help from our coaches.

You need curiosity to learn something new and a sprinkle of imagination!

Want to help? We are looking for volunteers and Rails coaches. Email us.

FAQ

How much does the workshop cost? Nothing, it's free! You just need to be excited!

Who is this aimed for? Women of any age with basic knowledge of working with a computer. We’ve had people of all ages taking part. Most of the speeches are given in Romanian, but small group work is done in English.

I know how to program - How can I help? We’re also looking for people to be mentors. We’ll have a two-three hour workshop before the event to walk you through the curriculum. Email us
