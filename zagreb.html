Rails Girls Zagreb, 19 - 20th October 2012

Rails Girls stižu u Zagreb, 19. i 20. listopada 2012. Pridružite nam se na besplatnim dvodnevnim radionicama programiranja iz Ruby on Rails programskog jezika. Ukoliko biste voljele naučiti kako napraviti svoju internetsku stranicu ili web aplikaciju te imate svoje prijenosno računalo, bit će nam zadovoljstvo uvesti vas u zabavan svijet programiranja uz naše mentore koji će vam biti na raspolaganju. Dobrodošli ste i ako već znate programirati u Ruby on Rails, a htjeli biste prisustvovati na našoj radionici i osvježiti znanje. Na našim radionicama mogu prisustvovati i muškarci, ali samo uz pratnju ženske osobe koja će prisustvovati radionicama, uz prethodnu potvrdu organizatora s obzirom na ograničen broj mjesta.

Rails Girls is coming to Zagreb, Croatia on 19 - 20th October 2012: join the two-day crash-course to the exciting world of building web applications with Ruby on Rails.

Apply now! Applications are open until October, 16 at midnight.

FAQ

How much does the workshop cost? Nothing, it's free! You just need to be excited!

Who is this aimed for? Women of any age with basic knowledge of working with a computer. We’ve had people of all ages taking part. Most of the speeches are given in english, but small group work is done in german. Please bring your laptop.

Can men attend? Yes, but you need to be accompanied by an interested lady. Also, girls are given a priority.

I know how to program - How can I help? We’re also looking for people to be coaches. We’ll have a two-three hour workshop before the event to walk you through the curriculum. Email us
