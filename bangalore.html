Frequently Asked Questions

Who can attend this workshop? Rails Girls is open to all women regardless of age, experience, or background. Whether you are a college student looking to explore a career in tech, a woman who is passionate about getting into the industry, or a woman who has taken a break from her career and wants to learn something new, then Rails Girls is the perfect event for you.

How much does the workshop cost? It's a free one day workshop. We've got you covered with lunch and snacks for the day.

How many spots are available? We have limited spots available for the workshop, so be sure to register early to secure your spot.

Do I need any prior programming experience to attend the workshop? Not at all! The workshop is designed for beginners, so no prior experience is necessary. All you need is a willingness to learn and a desire to explore the world of web development.

Will I get hands-on coding experience at the workshop? Absolutely! You'll be diving right into coding during the workshop and building your own web application. It's a great way to get a feel for what it's like to be a developer and work with Ruby on Rails.

Who will be leading the workshop? The workshop will be led by experienced mentors who are passionate about teaching and helping women succeed in tech. They will be available throughout the event to answer any questions and provide guidance.

What happens after registration? Kudos! You will receive a confirmation message about your spot in the workshop. Keep an eye on your email inbox for reminders and instructions about the workshop.

Can men attend? Men are welcome to attend as a plus one with a female friend.

I know how to program - How can I help?We’re also looking for people to be coaches. We’ll have a two-three hour workshop before the event to walk you through the curriculum. Also, don't forget to sign up as a coach.
