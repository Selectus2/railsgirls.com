Rails Girls Bucharest

was a free, friendly, exuberant, hands-on workshop for girls and women who want to get a glimpse of web development. .

On May 30-31, 2014, 31 motivated participants were trained by 16 enthusiastic coaches, supported by 6 organizers and everything was possible due to our wonderful sponsors.

Twitter: @RailsGirlsBuc, Facebook: RailsGirls Bucharest

FAQ

How much does the workshop cost? Nothing, it's free! You just need to be excited!

Who is this aimed for? Women of any age with basic knowledge of working with a computer and good command of English. Most of the materials are in English, the talks will be given in Romanian. Please bring your laptop.
