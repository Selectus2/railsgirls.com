FAQ

How much does the workshop cost? $10. This is just to help cover expenses for the event.

Who is this intended for? Women of any age with basic knowledge of working with a computer. We’ve had people of all ages taking part. Please bring your laptop - if you don't have one please email us and we'll help procure one for you.

Can men attend? Sure, but you need to be accompanied by an interested lady. Also, girls are given priority in the application process.

I know how to program - How can I help? We’re also looking for people to be coaches. We’ll have a two-three hour workshop before the event to walk you through the curriculum. Email us

Why are you doing this? We believe that the gender imbalance in programming is a Bad Thing™. This is our small attempt at getting more women invovled, because when programming is more diverse, programming is more awesome.
