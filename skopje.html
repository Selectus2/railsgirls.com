Rails Girls Skopje 2020 10-11 April

Hello Skopje!

During the free workshop we'll explore the magical world of Ruby on Rails, and build an application together.

Applications are now open!

Coaches who want to teach, please apply here!
Girls who want to learn, please apply here!

Follow us on Twitter, Facebook or Instagram for the latest updates!

You learn designing, prototyping and coding with the help from our coaches.

You need your own laptop, curiosity and a sprinkle of imagination!

Want to help? We are looking for volunteers & Rails coaches. Email us.

FAQ

Is this going to be in English or Macedonian?
Both :) Depending on the speakers and coaches that will support this event. We will try to include as many Macedonian speakers and coaches as we can.

How much does the workshop cost?
Nothing, it's free! You just need to be excited!

Who is this aimed for?
Women of any age with basic knowledge of working with a computer. We’ve had people of all ages taking part. Most of the materials are in English, but small group work is done in Macedonian. Please bring your laptop.

Can men attend?
Men are most welcomed as a participants' +1.

I know how to program - How can I help?
Applications coming soon!

Where it will be?



Address: 3rd Macedonian Brigade Blvd 56, Skopje




Agenda

Friday, April 10

19:00 Opening
19:30 Installation party & Dinner

Fun evening with snacks & drinks! Great opportunity for networking and getting to know the other students and coaches. Please bring your laptop so we can install Ruby on Rails for you.
21:00 Closing

Location: Web Factory

Saturday, April 11

9:00 Registration & Coffee

Saturday is the hands-on workshop day. We kick off the day with light breakfast and coffee. You will need the fuel to make those brain cells working hard! Make sure you tell us in your application if you have any special diatery requirements.
10:00 [Opening] Marta Arsovska Tomovska - Former Minister for Information Society and Public Administration

10:30 Welcome & Sponsors Presentations

We announce the schedule for the day and our sponsors will introduce themselves and give brief presentations. We will then split everyone into pairs and assign coaches to start the hands-on workshop.
11:00 Hands-on, Workshop Part 1

Get to know Ruby on Rails with some exercises on TryRuby and kick-start your first web application on Rails with this guide.
12:30 Lunch & Lightning Talks

Our coaches will give presentations on their backgrounds and involvements in the software engineering world. We make sure this is a great mix of people with various knowledge and expertise. You will hear a range of short and exciting talks from juniors to seniors.

We will have lunch provided but please make sure you tell us in your application if you have any special diatery requirements.
13:30 Workshop Part 2

Continue working on your web application with your coach. We dedicate this time to really get your head around how to code and make your app the way you want it to be. It takes a lot of concentration but it's super fun! We will have plenty of coffee and sugar to power you through this part of the day.
17:00 Showcase & Feedback session

Show off to each other what you've been working on all day!
18:00 Afterparty!

Location: TBA!
Sponsors, family, friends, partners and developers that happen-to-be-in-the-neighborhood are more than welcome to join.
