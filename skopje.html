FAQ

Is this going to be in English or Macedonian?
Both :) Depending on the speakers and coaches that will support this event. We will try to include as many Macedonian speakers and coaches as we can.

How much does the workshop cost?
Nothing, it's free! You just need to be excited!

Who is this aimed for?
Women of any age with basic knowledge of working with a computer. We’ve had people of all ages taking part. Most of the materials are in English, but small group work is done in Macedonian. Please bring your laptop.

Can men attend?
Yes, but you need to be accompanied by an interested lady. Also, girls are given a priority.
