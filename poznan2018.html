FAQ

How much does the workshop cost? Nothing, it's free! You just need to be excited!

Who is this aimed for? Women of any age with basic knowledge of working with a computer. We’ve had people of all ages taking part. The workshops are run primarily in Polish (unless there are non-Polish speaking attendees in groups) but knowing some English certainly helps.

How do you choose the attendees? We want a diverse group of people who are motivated and curious about learning.

What do I need to attend? A healthy amount of curiosity and your own laptop. That's all! We provide the venue, food and coffee :)

Can men attend? Yes, but you need to be accompanied by an interested lady. Also, girls are given a priority.

I know how to program - How can I help? We’re also looking for people to be coaches.

See more Q&A at our Facebook page and Code of Conduct.
