FAQs

How much does the workshop cost?
Overall Event costs INR 150. You don't have to worry about food. Your Lunch and evening snacks is on us and we guarantee you will get some nice gifts along with great learning experience!
Is on spot registration available?
Yes! We have spot registration.
Men's/ Boy's can attend this workshop?
Yes! If you are accompanied by Women.
Who is this aimed for?
Women of any age with basic knowledge of working with a computer. We’ve had people of all ages taking part.
I know Rails - How can I help?
We’re also looking for people to be coaches. We’ll have a half hour workshop before the event date to walk you through the curriculum. Join Us!
