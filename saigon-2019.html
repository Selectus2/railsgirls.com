FAQ

How much does the workshop cost?

Nothing, it's free! You just need to be excited!

Who is this aimed for?

Women of any age with basic knowledge of working with a computer. We’ve had people of all ages taking part. Most of the speeches are given in English, but small group work is done in Vietnamese. Please bring your laptop.

Can men attend?

Sorry, this event is for women only. But men can join as mentor if you have experience about Ruby on Rails.

I know how to program - How can I help?

We’re also looking for people to be coaches. We’ll have a two-three hour workshop before the event to walk you through the curriculum. Email us!
