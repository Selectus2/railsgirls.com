FAQ

How much does the workshop cost?
Overall Event costs 125 Rs. You don't have to worry about food. Your Lunch and evening snacks is on us and we gaurantee you will get some nice gifts along with great learning experience!

*Charges are for logistics.

Is on spot registration available?
Yes! But it will cost a bit high - 500 Rs.

Who is this aimed for?
Women of any age with basic knowledge of working with a computer. We’ve had people of all ages taking part.

Can men attend?
Yes, but as a paired with a female.

I know Rails - How can I help?
We’re also looking for people to be coaches. We’ll have a one hour workshop before the event to walk you through the curriculum. Register here
