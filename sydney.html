Rails Girls - Sydney July 9, 2022

Rails Girls is back in Sydney! During the free one-day workshop we’ll dive into the magical world of Ruby on Rails.

Tweet

We are offering two tracks:

For beginners: We'll dive into the magical world of Ruby on Rails. You'll learn designing, prototyping and coding with the help from our coaches. You don't need any previous coding experience. You do need your own laptop, curiosity and a sprinkle of imagination!

For not so beginners: Rails Girls Next, We'll be diving even deeper into programming in Ruby, covering topics like Test Driven Development, API's with the support of our wonderful coaches. To participate in this workshop, you're required to have either done the original Rails Girls day, or have some prior programming experience.

FAQ

How much does the workshop cost? Nothing, it’s free! You just need to be excited!

Who is this aimed at? Rails Girls Sydney is open to all adults (18+), but preference is given to those who identify as women.

Can men attend? Yes, but women are given priority.

Do you have a code of conduct? Yes, it is on the ruby.org.au website. We expect attendees, coaches, and sponsor representatives to behave respectfully to make sure everyone has a great time.
