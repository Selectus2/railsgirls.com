Rails Girls Organizer's Weekend

TERVETULOA!! Let's meet for real right after Frozen Rails in Helsinki

So many Rails Girls Chapters have evolved worldwide. Most of all those enthusiastic people organizing them never really met. Some months ago, the idea popped up, that a meeting of all Organizers and Coaches would be quite awesome.
Now, the idea became a plan:
The weekend of 13-14th of September 2014, right after Frozen Rails, is Rails Girls Organizer Weekend in Helsinki!
Come and join!

Kisko supports us by offering discount tickets for the conference for all Rails Girls Organizers and Coaches coming to Helsinki for that Weekend. Please indicate when you register for our Weekend whether you'd like to get one.

Why Helsinki?

After a little brainstorming, it became clear, that the most logic location for a first meeting is Finland, as it is the origin of the initative. Plus, it can be combined with visiting one of the most important Rails conferences.
So it's Helsinki, even though that means quite a trip for most of us, especially for the ones from other continents. But, who knows, maybe this can become a yearly thing, happening on different continents in the future?
