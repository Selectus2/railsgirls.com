Rails Girls Salt Lake16th-17th October 2015

Join us during this free two-day workshop where YOU will build a web app with Ruby & Ruby on Rails

You learn designing, prototyping and building your own web app with Ruby on Rails.

You need your own laptop, curiosity and a sprinkle of imagination!

Want to help? We are looking for volunteers and Rails coaches. Apply here.

Partners

Rails Girls Salt Lake City is co-organized with our awesome partners.

Want to help? We're looking for partners & sponsors for the non-profit event! Email us!

Devpoint Labs is a beginner focused accelerated software engineering school that offers full and part-time courses in downtown Salt Lake City. We help graduates achieve their professional goals and insert them into the best developer community.

Church & State is a 501 (c) (3) non-profit co-working and office space managed by the community. The entrepreneur center offers free space and resources with nothing expected in return.

FAQ

How much does the workshop cost? Nothing, it's free! You just need to be excited!

Who is this aimed for? Women of any age with basic knowledge of working with a computer. We’ve had people of all ages taking part. Please bring your laptop (Mac is preferred).

Can men attend? Yes, but you need to be accompanied by an interested lady. Also, girls are given priority.

I know how to program - How can I help? We’re also looking for people to be coaches. We’ll have a two-three hour workshop before the event to walk you through the curriculum. Apply here
