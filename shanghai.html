Partners

Rails Girls Shanghai is co-organized with our awesome partners.

Girls in Tech China makes us excited: they provide a platform on which women in technology can connect, empower and learn from one another.

Special shout-out to Daniel Lv, Ruby China and Shanghai on Rails for helping out in everything developer related!

The location and lunch will be provided by Sino-Finnish Center, the passion-based co-creation platform between Tongji and Aalto University.

FAQ

Who is this aimed for? Women of any age with basic knowledge of working with a computer. We’ve had people of all ages taking part. Most of the program is run in english and please bring your laptop.

How do you choose the attendees? We want a diverse group of people who are motivated and curious about learning.

Can men attend? Yes, but you need to be accompanied by an interested lady. Also, girls are given a priority.

I know how to program - How can I help? We’re also looking for people to be coaches. We’ll have a two-three hour workshop before the event to walk you through the curriculum. Email us
