Press Kit

Ruby on Rails Workshop for Girls in Helsinki November 26-27th, 2010

Over 100 interested girls signed-up for the workshop.
In 12 hours, 24 girls published 4 web applications.
An fun and educating weekend for women to get started with the world wide web.

Rails for Girls is a 24-hour Ruby-on-Rails workshop for absolute beginners in the world of programming and web development organized by the Helsinki-based web software development company Kisko Labs.

The weekend event is free and open to all enthusiastic girls and women. Rails for Girls wants to empower girls to build the capacity and acquire the tools to conquer the last online frontier.
