FAQ

How much does the workshop cost? Nothing, it's free! You just need to be excited, and don't forget to apply!

Do I need to be a student at the University of Ottawa? Who is this aimed for? Girls and Women from Ottawa High Schools and Universitis with basic knowledge of working with a computer. We’ve had people of all ages taking part in Rails Girls events across the world.

Can men attend too? Everyone is welcome to apply, although women are given priority. Keep an eye out for workshops coming in the Winter 2015 semester hosted by the uOttawa IEEE student branch!

I know how to program - How can I help? We’re also looking for people to be coaches. We’ll have a two-three hour workshop before the event to walk you through the curriculum. Email us!
