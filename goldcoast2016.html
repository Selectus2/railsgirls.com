Rails Girls Gold Coast

10 February 2016

Rails Girls comes to the Gold Coast! During this free one-day workshop we'll dive into the magical world of Ruby on Rails.

Apply now! Applications are open until 5th February 2016.

You learn designing, prototyping and coding with the help from our coaches.

You need your own laptop, curiosity and a sprinkle of imagination!

FAQ

How much does the workshop cost? Nothing, it's free! You just need to be excited!

Who is this aimed for? Women of any age with basic knowledge of working with a computer. We’ve had people of all ages taking part. Please bring your laptop or chromebook.

Can men attend? Yes, but you need to be accompanied by an interested lady. Also, girls are given a priority.
