Rails Girls SLC 21-22nd March 2014

Rails Girls comes to Salt Lake City, Utah! During the free two-day workshop we'll dive into the magical world of Ruby on Rails.

Apply now! Applications are open until the March 21.

You learn designing, prototyping and coding with the help from our coaches.

You need your own laptop, curiosity and a sprinkle of imagination!

Want to help? We are looking for volunteers and Rails coaches. Email us.

Get know everyone a little bit over a glass of wine and some treats. Bring your laptop if you can, and we’ll install Ruby on Rails for you. This is an open block of time so drop in whenever is good, and stay as long as you’d like!

FAQ

How much does the workshop cost? Nothing, it's free! You just need to be excited!

Who is this aimed for? Women of any age with basic knowledge of working with a computer. We’ve had people of all ages taking part. Most of the speeches are given in english, but small group work is done in german. Please bring your laptop.

Can men attend? Yes, but you need to be accompanied by an interested lady. Also, girls are given a priority.

I know how to program - How can I help? We’re also looking for people to be coaches. We’ll have a two-three hour workshop before the event to walk you through the curriculum. Email us
