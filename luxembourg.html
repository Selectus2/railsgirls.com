FAQ

How much does the workshop cost? Thanks to our sponsors, the workshop will be free of charge and food/drinks will be provided too. However, please bring your motivation, bags of enthusiasm and your own laptop to learn the basics of programming with us!

Who can participate? Girls and women from 15 years old as long as you are comfortable using a computer and the internet. We aim to gather a mix of fun-loving people from all backgrounds!

Can men attend? Yes they can, but girls will be given priority.

I know how to program. How can I help? We are looking for enthusiastic coaches to make this day a success! You are very welcome if you know how to program, even if you do not know Ruby on Rails yet or have no teaching experience. The workshop aims to make it easy for coaches too. We will show you!
