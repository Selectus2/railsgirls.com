Rails Girls Brussels, 20-21st October 2017

Hello Jeanneke!

On October 20-21st, Rails Girls comes back to Brussels! During this free two-day workshop, we'll dive into the chocolate world of HTML, CSS and Ruby on Rails!

Follow our Twitter account and like our Facebook page for updates!

Follow @RailsGirls_BE

You learn designing, prototyping and coding with help from our coaches.

You need your own laptop, curiosity and a sprinkle of imagination!

Want to participate? Applications are currently open :) To apply please fill in the application form by 14th October 2017. Good luck! :)

Friday, 20th October

18:00 - 19:30

Installation party

Get to know the attendees a little bit before hand. Bring your laptop, so we can install Ruby on Rails for you and with you.
19:30 - 21:00

Party-party

All of the participants and coaches are welcome to have a drink together and get to know each other better.

Saturday, 21st October

9:00 - 10:00

Welcome

Registration, breakfast and outline of the day.
10:00 - 12:00

Workshop

Jumpstart your first web page in HTML & CSS
12:00 - 13:00

Lunch & presentation of our coding partners
13:00 - 17:00

Workshop

Create your first Ruby on Rails application
17:00 - 18:00

Recap & follow-up

Share your feelings about the day and what you could do next. And last drink all together to wrap up the day! :)
