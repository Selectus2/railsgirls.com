FAQ

How much does the workshop cost? Nothing, it's free! You just need to brignt your laptop and be excited!

Who is this workshop for? Women of any age with basic knowledge of working with a computer. We’ve had people of all ages taking part. Talks will be conducted in English, but you are free to work in Swedish amongst your group (if all the group members speak Swedish). Please bring your laptop.

Can men attend? Yes, if we have available spots.
