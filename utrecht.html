Our awesome sponsors:

Inspire helps organizations to innovate with technology. Our solutions, such as web applications and mobile apps, make people smile. Throughout the process of conceiving, developing, launching and maintaining innovative technology, we always maintain an intense focus on our clients and their end users. Only when the solution has truly made a difference for them do we feel good about our work.

Firmhouse builds strong foundations for new ideas. Working in small, remote teams, they rapidly design and develop prototypes that gather meaningful data on new ideas — data used to make informed decisions on new products and businesses. From small experiments to MVPs, Firmhouse helps people get their ideas up and running. Firmhouse combines client work with their own products like intercityup , which makes Rails server management a snap.

FourStack designs, builds and hosts web based solutions, with a focus on quality, security and maintainability

Brightin is a team of designers and developers making beautiful web- and mobile apps.

Gitlab is open source software to collaborate on code. Manage git repositories with fine grained access controls that keep your code secure. Perform code reviews and enhance collaboration with merge requests. Each project can also have an issue tracker and a wiki. Used by more than 50,000 organizations, GitLab is the most popular solution to manage git repositories on-premises. And on GitLab Cloud you can host unlimited public and private repositories for free.

FAQ

How much does the workshop cost? Nothing, it's free! You just need to be excited!

Who is this aimed for? Women of any age with basic knowledge of working with a computer. We’ve had people of all ages taking part. Please bring your laptop.

Can men attend? Yes, but you need to be accompanied by an interested lady. Also, girls are given a priority.
