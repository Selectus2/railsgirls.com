FAQ

Who is this aimed for? Women of any age with basic knowledge of working with a computer. We’ve had people of all ages taking part. We speak English at our events. Please bring your laptop.

Can men attend? Yes, but you need to be accompanied by an interested lady. Also, girls are given a priority.
