ABOUT

Our aim is to demystify technology by jumping in and building something together. We provide a great experience in a friendly, helpful environment -- building a deep and lasting connection to the Rails & Vue.js communities.

This is a great workshop for anyone who
  • is interested in programming but doesn't know where to start
  • works at a tech company but isn't on the technical side and wants to communicate more effectively
  • is interested in checking out Ruby on Rails & Vue.js
  • is interested in getting some one-on-one coaching and gaining more confidence

FAQ

How much does it cost? Nothing, it's free!
You just need to be excited! And you need to show up ready to learn.
If you need to cancel please Contact Us at railsgirlsla@gmail.com There are limited spots.

What will I learn?
You'll learn designing, prototyping and coding with the help from our friendly coaches.

Who is this for?
The workshop is aimed
at beginners who want to learn to program.

Women/Girls/female-identifying people 13 years old and up with basic knowledge of working with a computer are encouraged to attend. Please spread the word to anyone you know who may be interested in attending.

Can men attend?Women/female-identifying people are given priority, but men are welcome as long as they are accompanied by an interested woman.

Can men Coach?
We love cool male coaches. We want to have a mix of male and female coaches.

What do I need? You need your own laptop, curiosity and a sprinkle of imagination!

How can I help?
We can always use your donations for future events.
