Rails Girls LA October 19th & 20th 2018

Rails Girls Los Angeles is part of Innovate Pasadena!
During this, free, two day workshop we'll dive into the magical world of Ruby & Ruby on Rails.
YOU will build a web application, learn about current programming tools and practices and get introduced to the world of technology with the support of a friendly coach and partner. No prior programming knowledge is necessary.

ABOUT

Our aim is to demystify technology by jumping in and building something together. We provide a great experience with a friendly, helpful environment and build a deep and lasting connection to the Rails Community.

This is a great workshop for anyone who
  • is interesting in programming but doesn't know where to start
  • works at a tech company but doesn't work on the technical side and wants to communicate more effectively
  • is interested in checking out Rails
  • is interested in getting some one on one coaching and gaining more confidence

FAQ

How much does it cost? Nothing, it's free!
You just need to be excited! And you need to show up ready to learn.
If you need to cancel Contact Us. There are limited spots.

What will I learn?
You'll learn designing, prototyping and coding with the help from our friendly coaches.

Who is this for?
The workshop is aimed
at beginners to programming. Women/Girls/female-identifying people 13 years old and up with basic knowledge of working with a computer are encouraged to attend. Please spread the word to anyone you know who may be interested in attending.

Can men attend?
Women/female-identifying people are given priority, but men are welcome as long as they are accompanied by an interested woman.

Can men Coach?
We love cool male coaches. We want to have a mix of male and female coaches.

You do I need? You need your own laptop, curiosity and a sprinkle of imagination!

How can I help?
We can always use your donations for future events.
