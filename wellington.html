Rails Girls Wellington Sat 22th & Sun 23th April 2017

Kia ora!

Rails Girls comes to Wellington! During the free two-day workshop we'll dive into the magical world of Ruby on Rails.

We are running a workshop during the weekend of April 22 & 23.

All of the material is available for you to learn at home by yourself but Rails Girls offers a casual and friendly environment to learn with others and a good tutor to student ratio to help get past some tricky areas.

Applications will open soon! Follow us on Facebook or subscribe to our mailing list to keep in the know.

You learn designing, prototyping and coding with the help from our coaches.

You need your own laptop, curiosity and a sprinkle of imagination!

FAQ

How much does the workshop cost? Nothing, it's free! You just need to be excited! And we can help out with public transport costs if necessary.

Who is this aimed for? People with non-binary gender identities or who identify as a girl or women, with basic knowledge of working with a computer. All ages may attend, but we would prefer very young people to come with a parent or guardian. Please bring your laptop.

Can men attend? Yes, but you need to be accompanied by an interested woman. Also, girls are given a priority.

Do you have a code of conduct? Yes – it is here. We expect all attendees, coaches, and sponsor representatives to behave respectfully to make sure everyone has a great time.
