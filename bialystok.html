FAQ

How much does the workshop cost? Nothing, it's free! You just need to be excited!

Who is this aimed for? Women of any age with basic knowledge of working with a computer. We’ve had people of all ages taking part. The speeches and small group work are done in polish, but training materials are in english. Please bring your laptop.

How do you choose the attendees? We want a diverse group of people who are motivated and curious about learning. We don't require any prior programming experience so go ahead and apply :)
