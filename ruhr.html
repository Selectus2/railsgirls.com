Rails Girls Ruhr

October 26th, 2019

Rails Girls is coming to Dortmund! A non-profit event for women of all ages to give them a great first experience in software craftsmanship and inspire a confidence to start building things on their own. With one dedicated coach per two or three learners, students will build a web application, from concept to code. Learn sketching, prototyping, basic programming and get introduced to the world of technology.

Are you ready to get coding? Then sign up here!

FAQ

Where can I sign up as an attendee?
RSVP right here!

How much does the workshop cost?
Nothing, it's free! You just need to be excited!

Do I need a laptop?
Yes! Please bring it with you :)

Can men attend?
Yes, but girls are given a priority.

Who is this aimed for?
Women of any age with basic knowledge of working with a computer. We’ve had people of all ages taking part. The workshop will be in English, but there will be coaches who speak German and a German version of the tutorial as well.

Can I help out as a coach? Yes you can! Make sure to sign up here for that! No previous knowledge of Ruby or Rails is necessary, experience with web programming is enough.

Girls* Although we use "girls" or "women" in our name, we welcome people who identify as female of any age, ethnicity and background. No matter if you’re still in school, don’t work with tech, have been working for many years or stay at home. Girls isn't meant to be cutesy or ageist :)
